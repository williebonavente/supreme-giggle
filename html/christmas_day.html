Christmas Day in the Philippines

A season of joy, faith, and family

Introduction

Christmas in the Philippines is celebrated with vibrant festivities, family reunions, and religious activities. Known for having one of the longest Christmas seasons in the world, Filipinos start celebrating as early as September.

Traditional Celebrations

Christmas is marked by "Simbang Gabi," a series of dawn masses, festive decorations, and Noche Buena, a midnight feast shared with loved ones. The holiday season is deeply rooted in family and faith.
