Bonifacio Day

Celebrating the life and bravery of Andres Bonifacio

Introduction

Bonifacio Day, celebrated every November 30, honors the birth and heroism of Andres Bonifacio, the "Father of the Philippine Revolution." Bonifacio’s courage and leadership sparked the revolution for Philippine independence.

Legacy of Bonifacio

Ceremonies and gatherings are held nationwide to honor Bonifacio’s contributions to the country's freedom. Bonifacio Day serves as a reminder of the sacrifices made for Philippine independence.
